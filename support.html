Common questions

Does Clausemate work without an internet connection?

Yes. The app is designed to run fully on-device. You can use the scanner, summaries, Ask chat, compare, and markup tools in airplane mode.

Why is my summary showing in English when I picked another language?

Version 1.0 ships with an English summary template. The UI is translated into 16 languages but the AI-generated summary body stays in English until you opt into the optional on-device multilingual model (approximately 3 GB) from the Profile tab. Without that download, the app runs perfectly in English.

How do I cancel a Monthly Supporter subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID, not through Clausemate. Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions, find Clausemate, and tap Cancel Subscription. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Apple handles all refunds. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Clausemate purchase, and request a refund. Clausemate itself never processes payments.

Did I buy the wrong tier?

Clausemate Pro has three tiers: a one-time "Thanks tip", a lifetime "Pro supporter", and a "Monthly supporter". None of them gates content — Clausemate is fully functional without any of them. If you bought one you didn't mean to, use Apple's refund flow above.

My scanned contract didn't give me good results — what do I do?

Good scans need decent lighting and pages held flat. Apple's document scanner works best on non-glossy paper with minimal glare. If a particular document is tricky, try the "Use sample text instead" option in the scan view to test that the rest of the flow works, and then rescan the tricky page.

Is Clausemate legal advice?

No. Clausemate is educational. It gives you general information about what your contract says and flags clauses that people commonly overlook. It is not legal advice tailored to your situation. For decisions that materially affect you, consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.

How do I delete everything?

Profile tab → Your data → Delete everything. This removes every document, clause, summary, and Ask entry from this iPhone. Because nothing is stored on any server, this is also a complete deletion from our point of view.

The "Restore Purchase" button doesn't find my purchase.

Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on this iPhone that you used when you originally purchased. Restore only surfaces purchases tied to the current Apple ID.
